EUROLAM by Bansal Sales Corporation Pvt Ltd is a pioneer in distribution of variety of interior building materials. The brand is associated with multiple globally renowned brands from countries such as Germany, South Korea, Portugal, Thailand and Turkey. The company was founded as a small timber merchant in 1978, but gradually over the years it has grown into a PAN INDIAN brand with multiple warehouses in all parts of the country. With new products being introduced every 90 days, our extensive selection of different surface materials meets all of the demands of our customers. We have created a company that is outfitted with a practical range, well suited to provide opulent environments and precise edging details.
