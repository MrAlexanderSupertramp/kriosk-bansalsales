VIVRE
PVC Foam Board

The construction industry uses conventional materials like plywood or wood which cause deforestation and other different types of pollution. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is a wood-based hybrid material, consisting of polymer fibers, calcium carbonate, lubricants and other chemicals. Unlike wood, it is completely water proof and borer-termite proof.

As one of the leading PVC players in India, Vivre PVC specializes in high quality wood plastic composite products by harnessing modern production lines, latest extrusion technology and skilled technicians.


Features

  • SGS Tested
  • 100% Termite Proof
  • 100% Water Proof
  • 100% Formaldehyde, Phenol and Toxic Free
  • Fire Retardant
  • Clean and Odour-less
