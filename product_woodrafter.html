Woodrafter is where craftsmanship meets contemporary design. This collection of louvred wall panels brings warmth, rhythm, and texture to interiors — offering a timeless wood aesthetic with modern modularity.

Ideal for accent walls, partitions, and ceiling applications, Woodrafter panels create a play of light and shadow that adds depth and dimension to any space. Designed for easy installation and available in versatile finishes, they bring both function and finesse to residential and commercial settings.

Elevate your space — the Woodrafter way.
